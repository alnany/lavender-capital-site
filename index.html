Our Mandate

Three areas where we operate.

Our engagements are selective and relationship-driven. The most significant mandates are handled privately and shared only with qualified counterparties. If any of the three areas below is relevant to you, reach out directly.

01 M&A Advisory & Brokerage

Acquisition and divestiture of digital asset businesses

We source, structure, and broker acquisitions across the full spectrum of regulated and crypto-native digital asset businesses. Buy-side and sell-side mandates handled with institutional discretion.

Coverage: licensed crypto exchanges and payment platforms, staking and node infrastructure firms, licensed financial infrastructure (EMI, PI, MSB, VASP), DeFi protocols and Layer 1/2 projects, SaaS and trading tooling businesses, media, iGaming, and frontier technology companies.

→ The most significant mandates are handled privately and shared only with qualified buyers. Contact us to request access to current deal flow.
02 Tokenization & Pre-IPO Structuring

Alternative asset access for retail and institutional investors

We work with an SFC-licensed platform in Hong Kong to bring alternative assets — including Pre-IPO tokens and structured equity positions — to retail and institutional investors.

  • Pre-IPO token brokerage via SFC-licensed HK platform, with distribution through top-10 CEX partnerships already in place.
  • GP liquidity solutions — working with GPs seeking to monetise a portion of pre-IPO positions via Layer 1 SPV structures.
  • End-to-end compliance layering: platform approval, SFC and distribution entity coordination, and full regulatory sign-off.

All structures are designed to be legal, compliant, and distribution-ready.

→ Reach out to discuss specific assets or mandates.
03 OTC, Fundraising & Capital Markets

Secondary positions, liquidity solutions, and selective fundraising

OTC & Secondary Markets: We engage directly with buyers and sellers of digital asset positions — counterparties include custodians, family offices, hedge funds, and Web3 native holders.

Positions covered: OTC block trades, SAFTs, SAFEs, private equity, and locked token allocations. Structured liquidity solutions for foundations, large token holders, and projects across our provider network.

Selective Fundraising: We take on fundraising engagements where our network creates a genuine strategic advantage and the relationship has long-term potential beyond the raise.

Fundraising scope: strategy and transaction narrative, financial model review, investor targeting and outreach, term sheet review and negotiation, round structuring and closing coordination.

→ Engagements are selective. Reach out with your situation — we will let you know quickly whether there is a genuine fit.
