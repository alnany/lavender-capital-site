M.02 Tokenization & Pre-IPO Structuring

We work with an SFC-licensed platform in Hong Kong to bring alternative assets, including Pre-IPO tokens and structured equity positions, to retail and institutional investors.

  • Pre-IPO token brokerage via SFC-licensed HK platform, with top-10 CEX distribution in place.
  • GP liquidity solutions via Layer 1 SPV structures for monetising pre-IPO positions.
  • End-to-end compliance layering: platform approval, SFC coordination, full regulatory sign-off.

All structures are designed to be legal, compliant, and distribution-ready.

M.03 OTC, Fundraising & Capital Markets

OTC & secondary markets: direct engagement with buyers and sellers of digital asset positions. Counterparties include custodians, family offices, hedge funds, and Web3-native holders. OTC block trades, SAFTs, SAFEs, private equity, and locked token allocations.

Selective fundraising: strategy and transaction narrative, financial model review, investor targeting and outreach, term sheet negotiation, and closing coordination. We take engagements where our network creates genuine strategic advantage.
